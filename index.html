About

An engineer's discipline, applied past the point most engineers stop.

Grace is a registered Electrical Engineer (PEng-T) with six years of experience in MEP systems — lighting, power, data, fire protection, HVAC, and drainage — coordinated through Building Information Modeling for EPC contractors, consultants, and property owners across Tanzania.

Most engineering work ends at handover. Grace's doesn't. Watching how buildings actually get maintained after the drawings are signed off led her to found Gracing Ventures — building facility asset management software that treats maintenance, compliance, and asset lifecycle as seriously as the original design.

Standards-compliant. Coordinated across disciplines. Documented properly. The same principles, applied to a building's entire working life, not just its construction.
